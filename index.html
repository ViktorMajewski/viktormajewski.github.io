About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Waterloo under the supervision of Prof. Spiro Karigiannis. Starting in April 2027, I will be a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Regensburg under the supervision of Prof. Bernd Ammann.

I obtained my PhD at the Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin under the supervision of Prof. Thomas Walpuski. I was a member of the Berlin Mathematical School (BMS) and a student representative.

My research interests are differential geometry, geometric analysis, and algebraic geometry. More specifically, I am interested in higher-dimensional gauge theories and the construction of enumerative invariants for special holonomy spaces.

I completed my Bachelor's and Master's degrees at the Georg-August-Universität Göttingen under the supervison of Prof. Victor Pidstrygach.
