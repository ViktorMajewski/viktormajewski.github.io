About Me

I am a PhD student at Humboldt Universität zu Berlin under the supervision of Prof. Thomas Walpuski. I am member of the Berlin Mathematical School (BMS) and a former student representative.

My research interests are differential geometry, geometric analysis and algebraic geometry. More presicely I am interested in higher dimensional gauge theories and the construction of enumerative invariants for special holonomy spaces.

I completed my undergraduate degree at the Georg-August-Universität Göttingen under the supervison of Prof. Victor Pidstrygach.
